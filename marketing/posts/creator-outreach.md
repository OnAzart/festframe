# Creator and fan-page outreach

Target small Tomorrowland preparation accounts, travel-group admins, DJs playing W2, and fan pages already discussing clashes. A real placement from a small relevant account is more useful than a large generic audience.

## First message

> Hey - I built a free Tomorrowland planner after testing it during W1. The useful bit is the output: the whole route becomes a lock-screen wallpaper, so people do not keep reopening the timetable.
>
> W2 is ready now. I am not looking for a scripted endorsement; I can send you a 10-second demo and a tagged link if it feels useful for your audience.

## When they say yes

> Thanks. Here is the short demo and your link:
>
> https://festframe.vercel.app/?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=creator&utm_campaign=w2_launch&utm_content=creator_name
>
> The cleanest angle is: `your whole W2 route on your lock screen`. Please mention that it is free and unofficial; your own wording is better than a script.

## One follow-up

> Quick follow-up because W2 starts Friday. No pressure if it is not a fit - I can also generate a route example for your actual Friday picks so you can judge the output first.

Do not follow up more than once. Do not ask for a positive review; ask them to use it or ignore it.
