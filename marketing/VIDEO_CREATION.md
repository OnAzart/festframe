# Make three videos in 15-25 minutes

Do not generate an AI festival scene. The product output is already the strongest creative, and a real phone recording is more credible.

## What you need

- an iPhone screen recording;
- one finished FestFrame lock-screen screenshot;
- CapCut, Instagram Reels, or TikTok editor;
- the three hooks from [`posts/instagram-tiktok.md`](./posts/instagram-tiktok.md).

## Record once

1. Turn on Do Not Disturb and hide notifications.
2. Open FestFrame in Safari in a clean tab and skip email.
3. Start on W2 Friday in Board view.
4. Start screen recording.
5. Tap one artist, change another to Must, and add a Maybe.
6. Open My Schedule for about one second.
7. Tap Export and show the wallpaper preview.
8. Stop recording. Do not show an email address or personal notification.

Record slowly. Editing can speed the middle up; it cannot repair taps that are impossible to follow.

## Build the 10-second master

| Time | Visual | Text |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0.0-1.2s | Finished lock-screen screenshot | One variant hook |
| 1.2-4.5s | Three priority taps | `Must / Want / Maybe` |
| 4.5-6.0s | My Schedule or visible overlap | `See the clashes` |
| 6.0-8.8s | Export preview | `Export wallpaper or calendar` |
| 8.8-11.0s | Finished lock screen again | `Plan W2 free · FestFrame` |

Use hard cuts. No intro animation, voiceover, avatar, stock footage, or feature list.

## Export settings

- 1080 × 1920, 9:16.
- H.264 MP4, 30 fps.
- Keep text inside the central 80% so platform controls do not cover it.
- Use one sans-serif font, white text, and a dark 50-70% backing only when the wallpaper is too busy.
- Keep `MADE WITH FESTFRAME` visible in the final shot.

## Create the three variants

Duplicate the master project twice. Change only the opening text and caption:

1. Problem: `Stop reopening the timetable all day`.
2. Visual: `Your whole Tomorrowland route. One glance.`
3. Proof: `156 wallpapers exported during W1`.

This makes the result interpretable. Three different recordings would test production differences instead of message differences.

## Music

Export the master without copyrighted music. Add a quiet trending sound inside Instagram or TikTok when publishing. The visual must still make sense muted.

## Fast static fallback

If recording fails, publish a two-slide carousel:

1. Real lock-screen screenshot with `Your W2 route. One glance.`
2. Export preview with `Free · unofficial · email optional` and the URL.

The carousel is sufficient for Stories, community posts, and a Reddit image post.
