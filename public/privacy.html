Privacy

Effective 22 July 2026

FestFrame is an independent personal project operated online as OnAzart. This notice explains the limited information used to run the planner.

Email is optional. You can skip it and keep your plan only in this browser. FestFrame sends marketing or product-update email only when you explicitly opt in.

Information we process

  • If you enter an email: the normalized email address, a hashed lookup key, the country code supplied by Vercel, your selected priorities, weekend, wallpaper theme, and whether you opted in to occasional product-update emails.
  • If you use the planner: an anonymous session identifier, a persistent browser identifier, product actions such as opening the planner or exporting a wallpaper, festival date/weekend, country code, and limited acquisition fields such as UTM campaign values and referring hostname.
  • On your device: the browser identifier, a local profile identifier, and your current plan, used to keep the planner working between visits. If you submit email, the browser identifier may also be associated with that optional email lead.

FestFrame does not intentionally store raw IP addresses. Artist selections are stored with an email profile only when you choose email restore; they are not sent to advertising platforms.

Why we use it

We process this information to provide and restore festival plans, generate exports, maintain security and reliability, understand whether the product is useful, and measure which launch channels lead to meaningful use.

Service providers

Vercel hosts the app, supplies coarse country information, and provides aggregated, cookieless Web Analytics. Neon, part of Databricks, stores product events, optional email leads, and email-linked plans. Ko-fi receives information only when you follow the optional support link. Each provider processes information under its own terms and privacy notice.

Retention and control

Data is kept only while it is useful for operating and validating FestFrame. You may clear local data in your browser. To request access or deletion of an email profile, contact the operator through the OnAzart GitHub profile; do not post your email publicly in a GitHub issue.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, or object to processing, and to complain to your data protection authority. FestFrame currently relies on necessary service delivery and legitimate interests for the limited first-party product analytics described above.

Account security

Email restore is temporarily unverified. Anyone who knows the same email address may be able to load its saved plan. Do not store sensitive information in FestFrame.

Changes

This notice may change as FestFrame develops. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are published.

FestFrame is unofficial and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official service of Tomorrowland.
