# Wallpaper Generation Prompt

Use this as the master prompt for future background variants. The application should add all typography and schedule content after generation.

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: 9:16 smartphone lock-screen wallpaper background for an unofficial European electronic music festival schedule

Primary request:
Create an original premium fantasy world about consciousness, collective energy, music, and discovery. Use celestial mechanics, delicate botanical forms, refined metallic filigree, crystalline materials, and one small luminous focal point. The result should appeal to attendees of large European electronic-music festivals while remaining legally and visually distinct from Tomorrowland, its stages, logos, butterfly, book iconography, and the official Consciencia emblem.

Composition and safe areas:
- Exact vertical 9:16 composition.
- Keep the entire top 34% almost black, quiet, and low-detail for the iPhone clock and widgets.
- Keep a broad, dark central column from 36% to 92% height for a readable schedule overlay.
- Place ornamental detail along the far left and right edges.
- Put the brightest focal point below 72% height and keep it small.
- Maintain strong contrast behind white schedule text.
- The wallpaper must still look intentional when partially covered by translucent timetable blocks.

Style and finish:
Cinematic high-end 3D illustration with sophisticated European festival-poster craftsmanship, realistic metal and crystal, controlled detail, deep spatial atmosphere, and a collectible premium finish. Mysterious and euphoric rather than gothic or childish.

Palette:
Deep black and midnight navy base; restrained antique gold, coral red, turquoise, cyan, and small pearlescent-violet highlights. Avoid a one-color blue or purple composition.

Constraints:
No people. No text, letters, numbers, logo, watermark, UI, phone mockup, official mark, copied stage design, or bright details behind the clock area. Original artwork only.
```

Generated candidates:

- `celestial-gate.png` - ornate celestial gateway and iris portal
- `botanical-consciousness.png` - dark botanical cosmos and radiant seed
- `crystal-observatory.png` - futuristic crystalline observatory
- `consciousness-desert.png` - 2026-inspired surreal stone consciousness monument and golden path
